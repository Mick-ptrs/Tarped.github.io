SUSTAINABLE DESIGN

FREITAG’s approach is sustainable because it minimizes waste and reduces the need for new resources. By upcycling materials like used truck tarps, seat belts, and bicycle inner tubes, FREITAG transforms items that would otherwise end up in landfills into durable bags and accessories. This process not only prevents waste but also lowers the environmental impact associated with producing new raw materials.

Additionally, FREITAG’s focus on creating long-lasting products supports a circular economy. Items designed for durability reduce the need for frequent replacements, which helps cut down on overproduction and the energy-intensive processes of manufacturing and distribution. By valuing upcycling and durability, FREITAG promotes a model of consumption that respects resources and aligns with environmental sustainability.
