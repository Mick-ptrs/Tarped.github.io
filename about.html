ABOUT FREITAG

MAKING TARP TIMELESS

FREITAG is a Swiss brand known for its unique bags and accessories made from recycled truck tarps. Founded in 1993, the brand combines functionality with urban style, creating durable products that are as individual as the materials they’re crafted from. Each FREITAG piece carries a story, blending distinctive design with practical features to suit modern, on-the-go lifestyles. From bags to clothing, FREITAG’s pieces are made to last and stand out, merging rugged aesthetics with innovative, thoughtful design.

FROM TRUCK TO BAG

FREITAG bags are built with character and resilience, crafted from reclaimed materials that have already lived a full life on the road. Each tarp is carefully selected, cleaned, and cut to shape, ensuring every bag is truly one of a kind, bearing its own unique marks and colors. FREITAG’s skilled team then assembles the components with precision, merging functionality with a distinctive, urban aesthetic.
